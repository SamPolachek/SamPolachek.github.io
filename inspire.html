What Makes a Hero?

A hero is someone that does not give up even when that seems like the only option. A hero is someone who is willing to fight battles that seem unwinnable.

Someone who shows these qualities is NF, a rapper who has spent a majority of music talking about his battles with depression and a troubled mind. However, as shown in his most recent album "HOPE", NF has a new outlook on life and has managed to overcome much of what has troubled him.

Not only has NF used his music as a means of fighting battles against his inner demons, but his lyrics have also touched the hearts of those who understand and/or relate to what he goes through. NF has inspired many of his listeners to never give up and continue fighting.
